What is Circular Economy?

The Circular Economy is an innovative model aimed at reducing waste, maximizing resource efficiency, and fostering sustainability. It emphasizes reuse, repair, and recycling over the traditional 'take-make-dispose' system.

  • Reduce: Minimize energy and material use.
  • Reuse: Extend product life by repurposing.
  • Repair: Enhance product durability.
  • Innovate: Build sustainable systems and products.
